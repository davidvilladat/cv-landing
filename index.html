About

Aeronautical Engineer
& Data Scientist.

I'm Cristian Villada — Aeronautical Engineer and Data Scientist currently serving as Flight Crew Training Officer (DS) at Etihad Airways in Abu Dhabi. I design EBT/SBT digital training programmes, build BI solutions, and develop Python automations across a fleet of over 106 aircraft.

Before Etihad I led commercial strategy and pricing at TAAG Angola Airlines — building their Google Cloud data infrastructure and BI framework from scratch — and worked in ancillary revenue and fuel analytics at Viva Air Colombia.

My work sits at the intersection of flight operations, commercial intelligence, and applied machine learning. The tools on this site are side projects that turn that expertise into something tangible and useful for the wider aviation community.
